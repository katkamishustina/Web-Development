Genes involves food preferences

The University of Edinburgh published study that explains why people have different food preferences. People's eating habits are associated with their upbringing, but genes play a very important role too.

Cook for Ukraine

Ukrainian chef Olia Hercules and Russian chef Alissa Timoshkin have teamed up to create the 'Cook for Ukraine' - the initiative which, aims to gather donations for Ukrainian families. The fundraiser has raised over $280 000.
